JetBrains Runtime: Why we forked OpenJDK?

This talk is about the history and reasons for the creation of JetBrains Runtime, OpenJDK fork specialized for desktop applications.

We will discuss the problems IntelliJ IDEA developers encountered, and how they were resolved thanks to JetBrains Runtime.

You will hear about font rendering, antialiasing, HarfBuzz, HiDPI displays, and project Lanai.

Konstantin Bulenkov,
JetBrains
